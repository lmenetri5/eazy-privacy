Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Eazy French ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the Eazy French app and any related website or page we operate.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

Eazy French does not require account creation, and we do not ask you to provide your name, email address, or other similar personal information in order to use the app.

Learning progress and preferences stored locally

To make the app work properly, Eazy French stores certain information locally on your device or browser. This may include your selected avatar, lesson progress, topic scores, and completed quiz items.

Advertising-related information

On Android and iOS, the free version of Eazy French may display ads through Google AdMob. When ads are shown, Google and its partners may collect information such as device identifiers, IP address, approximate location, ad interactions, and diagnostic information in accordance with their own privacy practices.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide the app’s French-learning content and features.
  • To remember your local settings and progress.
  • To display ads in the free version of the app.
  • To maintain app functionality, performance, and stability.

3. What We Do Not Currently Do

We do not currently provide user accounts, cloud sync, direct in-app messaging, or our own analytics platform. We also do not currently process subscription payments or purchases directly inside the app.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We do not upload your learning progress to our own servers. If ads are displayed, information may be processed by Google AdMob and its partners for advertising, fraud prevention, and measurement purposes.

5. Data Retention

Data stored locally by the app generally remains on your device or browser until you clear app data, clear browser storage, or uninstall the app. Data processed by Google AdMob is retained according to Google’s own policies.

6. Your Choices

  • You can stop using the app at any time.
  • You can delete locally stored app data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.
  • You can manage advertising preferences through your device settings or Google settings where available.

7. Third-Party Services

Eazy French may use the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob / Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

8. Children’s Privacy

Eazy French is a general audience language-learning app. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children through account registration or similar forms. If you believe personal information has been provided to us directly, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date on this page.
